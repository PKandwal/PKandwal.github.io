How to Make Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun Recipe

Gulab jamun is a sweet confectionary or dessert, originating in the Indian subcontinent and a type of mithai popular in India, Pakistan, Nepal, the Maldives and Bangladesh, as well as Myanmar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup khoya (dried milk)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Oil for frying
  • Cardamom powder

Steps to Make Gulab Jamun

  1. Make the sugar syrup by heating water and sugar until it thickens slightly. Add cardamom powder.
  2. In a bowl, mix khoya, flour, baking powder, and ghee to form a dough.
  3. Shape into small balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried balls in sugar syrup for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.
