How to Make Laddoo

Ladoo Recipe

Ladoo is a traditional Indian sweet made from a variety of ingredients, often enjoyed during festivals and celebrations. The following recipe is for a basic Besan Ladoo, which is made from gram flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (e.g., almonds, cashews) (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water (optional, to adjust consistency)

Steps to Make Laddoo

  1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the besan to the pan and roast it in the ghee, stirring continuously. Roast until the besan turns golden brown and gives off a nutty aroma. This should take around 10-15 minutes.
  3. Once the besan is roasted, remove the pan from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the roasted besan, mixing well. If you want to add nuts, stir them in at this stage.
  5. If the mixture is too dry and crumbly, you can add a small amount of water to bind it together. Mix until the consistency is such that you can easily shape the mixture into balls.
  6. While the mixture is still warm, shape it into small round balls or laddoos using your hands. Let them cool completely and set.
  7. Store the ladoos in an airtight container. Enjoy them as a sweet treat or during special occasions!
