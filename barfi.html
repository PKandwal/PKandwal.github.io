How to Make Barfi

Barfi Recipe

Barfi, also known as Burfi, is a rich and delectable Indian sweet made from condensed milk, sugar, and various flavorings. It's a popular treat during festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (e.g., almonds, cashews) (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped pistachios for garnish (optional)

Steps to make Barfi

  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the milk and let it warm slightly. Then add the milk powder, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Once the mixture starts to thicken, add the condensed milk and continue to stir. Cook until the mixture begins to leave the sides of the pan and starts to form a soft dough-like consistency.
  4. Add cardamom powder and mix well. If using, add the chopped nuts at this stage and mix them into the mixture.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased plate or tray and flatten it using a spatula or the back of a spoon. Smooth the surface as needed.
  6. Allow the barfi to cool completely at room temperature. Once cooled, cut it into squares or diamond shapes.
  7. Garnish with chopped pistachios if desired. Serve and enjoy!
